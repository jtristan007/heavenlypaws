Hi, I'm Ariana, and I believe every dog deserves to feel their best.

From a childhood surrounded by farm animals and house pets, to volunteering at a grooming shop from age 11, working at the zoo for a number of years, working as a groomer, and walking dogs, animals have always been at the centre of my world.

I come directly to your home to deliver gentle acupressure massage in the environment where your dog feels safest. No new smells, no stressful car rides, just calm intentional healing in your living room.

I've dedicated myself to learning animal acupressure and the profound effect it has on our pets' health, happiness, and quality of life. I can't wait to share that with you and your furry family member.

Ariana

The Practice

Ancient healing, tailored for your dog.

Acupressure is the practice of applying gentle pressure to specific points on the body to stimulate mental, physical, and emotional wellbeing. It follows the same channels used in traditional Chinese medicine, activating points that improve the flow of Qi, the body's life energy.

For dogs, this means reduced stress, better circulation, relief from pain, and a deep sense of calm. It is completely safe, non-invasive, and requires no medication. Just touch, intention, and care.
